Back Story

You are crossing a bridge on cold rainy night when suddenly a black Jeep pulls up beside you.
Two guys get out from the car carrying a large bag on their shoulders.
One man is wearing a red hoodie with a light green face mask covering his face.
The other guy is wearing a white and black checkered shirt with a blue paterrned bandana on his face.
They throw the bag over the bridge into the river below.
Both of them quickly jump back into the Jeep and drive off.
You watch the Jeep drive off, it's number plate reads,
'DEF 1962'. You look over the bridge to see what they threw.
You are shocked to see a body floating in the water. Just as you are
looking over, a Police car pulls up beside you.


The police found the dead body and are suspecting you to be the killer since you were the only one found around the crime scene.
To prove your innocence, you must correctly answer the following questions by recalling details of the incident.


The interrogator asks: So, if you're not the culprit, then tell me about what really happened that night.
